John Alcock is a current masters student in jazz saxophone at the Guildhall school. He studied mathematics at the University of Manchester, during which time he performed with Steve Berry (Loose Tubes) and had shows in venues including Night and Day. Now based in London he has had performances at Oliver's jazz bar and the Vortex jazz club, as well as being a featured soloist in the Guildhall Big Band and Studio Orchestra.
