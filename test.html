    John Alcock is a jazz saxophonist, woodwind player and teacher based in London. A recent graduate of the Guildhall School of Music and Drama, John has performed at venues including the Vortex, the 606 Club, Oliver's jazz bar and Alfies Soho, both with his own band and in other musician's projects.

    He regularly does gigs, recording sessions and functions in and out of London, aswell as having experience teaching saxophone, flute and clarinet to all ages and abilities.
